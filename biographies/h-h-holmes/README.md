# H.H. Holmes & Detective Geyer Biography Series (бһҮбһёбһңбһ”бҹ’бһҡбһңбһҸбҹ’бһҸбһ· H.H. Holmes бһ“бһ·бһ„бһӣбҹ„бһҖбһҹбҹҠбһҫбһ”бһўбһ„бҹ’бһҖбҹҒбһҸ Geyer)

Welcome to the dedicated biography and analysis series on the infamous Gilded Age serial killer, H.H. Holmes, and the pioneer of scientific detection who brought him to justice, Detective Frank Geyer. This folder contains a comprehensive, bilingual (Khmer/English) collection of historical articles, psychological profiles, and creative adaptations.
